Avoid smoking

Smoking can realy harm your body- especially for the long run.

Drink water

Drinking water helps your body a lot- you should focus on drinking water and not sugary drinks.

It helps maximizing physical performance, improves your energy levels and brain function, and helps with a lot of health issues.

Eat vegetables and fruits

Vegetables and fruits are loaded with prebiotic fiber, vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, many of which have potent health effects.

Studies show that people who eat more vegetables and fruits tend to live longer and have a lower risk for heart disease, obesity, and other conditions.
